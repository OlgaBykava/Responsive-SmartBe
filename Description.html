Description:

Smart Bench ("SmartBe") could appear as a result of pressing a special button on a fence/a wall on the street. Therefore, sick/old people could have a rest.


How it works:

  1. Find

    a green button

    on the wall

  2. Press the button.

  3. Wait for some seconds while the bench is rolling out of the folder in the wall.

  4. Use the bench.


Caution:

When the bench is not used for more than 5 minutes, it rolls back into the folder.

Safe,useful and comfortable benches for everybody
